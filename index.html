About Us

XETU is a cutting-edge eRickshaw booking app created by us to improve urban mobility in our hometown. We’ve witnessed the challenges of local transportation and are committed to making life easier for our community. Our platform serves as a bridge between residents and local eRickshaw drivers, offering a seamless and efficient travel solution. Inspired by the Assamese word for "bridge," XETU aims to provide a smooth and accessible transportation experience while contributing positively to the society we care deeply about.

Plans

Our vision extends beyond Golaghat. We plan to expand XETU across Assam and eventually across India, bringing our innovative transportation solution to more communities. In the future, we aspire to take XETU global, making a positive impact on urban mobility around the world. Our commitment is to continuously enhance our app and adapt to the evolving needs of our users, ensuring a better travel experience for everyone.
